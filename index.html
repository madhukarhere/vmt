Who We Are

About Vivekananda Diagnostics

Vivekananda Diagnostics and Medical Research is a trusted, full-service diagnostic center located in the heart of Pedda Waltair, Visakhapatnam. We are committed to delivering accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic services to every patient who walks through our doors.

With state-of-the-art equipment, a team of qualified medical professionals, and a patient-centered approach, we offer everything from advanced MRI scans to routine blood tests — all under one roof.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive diagnostic solutions under one roof

Dental

Complete dental check-up, consultation, and treatment by qualified dentists.

Day Care

Short-stay observation, minor procedures, and same-day discharge facility.

MRI

High-resolution magnetic resonance imaging for brain, spine, and joints.

Ultrasound

Safe, real-time internal body imaging including abdomen and obstetric scans.

X-Ray

Quick digital radiography for chest, bones, and all standard X-ray needs.

OP Chambers

Outpatient consultation rooms with specialist doctors across departments.

Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy stocking medicines, dispensing prescriptions reliably.

Pathology Lab

Blood tests, urine analysis, cultures, biopsies, and all lab investigations.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation, pain management, and recovery for injuries and chronic conditions.

TMT

Treadmill cardiac stress test to evaluate heart health under exercise conditions.

Echo

Echocardiography for detailed imaging of the heart's structure and function.

ECG

Electrocardiogram to record heart rhythm and detect cardiac abnormalities.
